Amira Chriki Mourad

I am currently a researcher with the FUN project team at the Centre Inria de l'Université de Lille.
From April 2025 to May 2025, I was a vacataire in Computer Science at IMT Nord Europe, France.
From September 2020 to September 2023, I was an assistant professor at the Faculty of Science of Gabes, in Gabes, Tunisia.
In 2022, I obtained a PhD in Computer Science from the National School of Computer Sciences (ENSI), University of la Manouba, Tunisia.
My PhD was conducted in collaboration between the University of Technology of Troyes (UTT), France, and ENSI.
I carried out my research at the IResCoMath laboratory in Tunisia and the CapSec platform in Troyes.
In 2016, I received a Research Master's degree in Computer Science and Networks from the Faculty of Science of Gabes, Tunisia.
